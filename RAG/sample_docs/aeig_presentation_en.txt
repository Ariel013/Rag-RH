WHO WE ARE
----------
AEIG is a Pan-African educational and technological group specializing in digital training. Founded in 2018 in Cotonou, it brings together several complementary entities that cover the entire educational and professional journey in the field of information technology, particularly cybersecurity and computer science.

Our belief: Africa must train its own digital experts to build a sovereign and globally competitive future.

OUR MISSION
-----------
To train, innovate and connect the next generation of African digital talents by offering world-class programs in computer science, cybersecurity and emerging technologies.

OUR VISION
----------
To become the reference group for digital education in Sub-Saharan Africa by 2030, by developing talents capable of meeting the continent's and the world's technological challenges.

GROUP ENTITIES
--------------
AEIG is organized around several poles/companies:

1. EPITECH AFRICA         — Higher school of computer science and cybersecurity (Bachelor & Master)
2. FUTURE STUDIO          — School for creative digital and multimedia careers
3. DIGITAL VALLEY         — Hub for innovation, incubation and startup acceleration
4. AFRIKEDUTECH            — EdTech platform for online learning (e-learning)
5. PIE                    — Innovation and Entrepreneurship Pole
6. TAKABYTES              — Tech community media (digital content, podcasts, tech news)
